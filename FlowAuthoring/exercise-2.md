# Exercise 2

Since you plan to work quite a bit with FME Flow it might be good for you to have your own folder where you can save your workspace but also your data.

**Assignment**:

Login on the FME Flow and create a Repository for yourself.

Also create a folder for yourself where you can save data with your name.

<details>

<summary>Tips:</summary>

* What do you think happens with repositories regarding security? Can others also see your repository and everything that is in there?

- What would be the best place to create a folder for your data and why?

</details>

<details>

<summary>Bonus:</summary>

Look in the existing repositories and Resource folders. Some already contain workspaces and data. Try to figure out what they are and why they are there.

</details>
